August 2016: The Proceedings of the 54th Annual Meeting of the Association for Computational Linguistics, the co-located Proceedings of the Fifth Joint Conference on Lexical and Computational Semantics, Proceedings of The 20th SIGNLL Conference on Computational Natural Language Learning and 15 of the associated allied conferences and workshops co-located in Berlin are now available on the Anthology.

We have programmatically generated a single .bib file containing most (99%) of the 38K+ records in the ACL Anthology. Feel free to download it and suggest revisions. This is a beta service and the current state of the records may be missing information or have incorrect BibTeX. When you find errors, please report them to us. Also, you may want to read the 2016 Q3 report on the Anthology that was prepared for the ACL Executive Board.
