November 2016: The Proceedings of the 2016 Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing (EMNLP 2016) and its associated 7 workshops are now available in the Anthology. Additionally, the Proceedings of the 9th International Natural Language Generation Conference (INLG 2016), the INLG 2016 Workshop on Computational Creativity in Natural Language Generation, the Proceedings of the 19th Annual Conference of the European Association for Machine Translation, the Proceedings of the 2nd International Workshop on Natural Language Generation and the Semantic Web (WebNLG 2016) and the Proceedings of the 2016 and 2015 Deep Machine Translation Workshops are now available in the Anthology.
