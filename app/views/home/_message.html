Jan 2018:

All of the 2017 issues of the Computational Linguistics journal (CL) are now available in the ACL Anthology. Also, the Proceedings of the International Conference Recent Advances in Natural Language Processing (RANLP 2017) as well as its 5 workshops, and the Proceedings of the 29th Conference on Computational Linguistics and Speech Processing (ROCLING 2017), the Proceedings of the 8th Workshop on Cognitive Modeling and Computational Linguistics (CMCL 2018), the Proceedings of the Fourth International Workshop on Computatinal Linguistics of Uralic Languages and the Proceedings of the Society for Computation in Linguistics (SCiL) are also now available in the Anthology.

Due to a publishing error with the EMNLP 2017 proceedings, please note that the ACL Anthology identifiers for many of the EMNLP papers have changed. Please cite the appropriate version via the IDs in the current Anthology.
